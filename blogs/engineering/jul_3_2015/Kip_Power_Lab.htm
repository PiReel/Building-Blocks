Kip Power Project

The energy need of the future is the mission of the Kip Power Project. The projects core revolves around two questions.

1.)    What methods can we use to collect energy?

2.)    What can we use energy for?

Kip’s Power Project is all about communication, sharing, and taking the small ideas and making them real. If you understand the science of energy you’ll understand the questions I posed and there significance in a world full of energy.

If the Earth is to evolve past a collection of world civilizations, if we want to become more than Earth bound beings we need to realize the connections between society and nature. This fact has ignited a thought that will help answer the project’s core questions, this thought is:

“Society and its total amount of energy in sustaining itself is a part of the sum of the total amount of energy that is contained in a complete natural system.”

Ummmm...What? How is this helpful? Have you heard something like this before?

At Kip we call this the Social Energy Theory. We hope to create a foundation for science and engineering to flourish through this theory and through this project.
