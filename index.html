Kip's Head Qaurter welcomes you to our place on the web.

Based out of Omaha, NE we are pleased to present our website. We will continually be adding more to it to better serve the users of this site to the best of our ability.

Search our site by clicking our projects page link or our blogs link, we also have a quick links menu too if you know what project your interested in.

This year we are looking forward to building a relationship with our local community here in Omaha, NE, by reaching out to the Nebraska University system to contribute to science and engineering education for college students and graduating high school seniors. The main focus of our outreach is to concentrate on three areas which are:
  1. Teaching web technologies role in science and engineering.
  2. Teaching about alternative forms of transportation such as bicycling and electric assisted bicycling.
  3. Supporting university science and engineering clubs.
Please check out our page at University of Nebraska Outreach Project for more information.

We would also like to announce our project to support the W3C and the ECMA scripting standard. We currently have documentation regarding our efforts to help compatibility of HTML 5 standards and also are working on concepts for the future of the web and how it can be leveraged for science and engineering.
