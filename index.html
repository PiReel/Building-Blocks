Public Announcement for Peer Reviewers

Kip Omaha would like to make a public announcement. We are currently seeking people who would like to participate in an open peer review group. This peer group would be formed to discuss inline JSON and the djBase.js inline JSON javascript library. If your interested in volunteering please contact us by e-mail ( kipOmaha@gmail.com ) or by using one of the methods listed from the "Contact Kip Omaha" button. Participants need not be local to Omaha, NE.

Although this is strictly a volunteer position we are looking for good people to work with so please take your time and see if this is something you want and can do.

  1. Eduction: High School Diploma or G.E.D.
  2. Expierence: One or more years prefered but not required.
  3. Availability: No set work schedule but a reasonable deadline must be met.

The position requires you to be critical and detialed oriented. The tasks involved are:

  1. Familiarizing yourself with JSON, inline JSON, and the djBase.js library.
  2. Writing an open response letter regarding your thoughts on inline JSON.
  3. Writing an open response letter regarding your thoughts on javascript libraries that work with JSON.
  4. Writing an open response letter regarding your thoughts on javascript regExp parsers.
  5. Writing an open response letter regarding your thoughts on the libraries addBoxSet() method.
  6. Writing an open response letter regarding your thoughts on the libraries addIntervalListener method.

Upon completion of the task you will recieve a voucher for a free log in to the Kip Omaha Dev Team Application Portal. *
Thank you for taking your time to read Kip's public announcement.
Regards, Kip from Omaha.

* :   The log on will be awarded based on the terms and conditions of the agreement for the volunteer position.
