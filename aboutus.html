On this page you can find information regarding everything Kip is doing or has done.
For our start up year, 2016, we have lots of exciting things in development. Here is what
our timeline for the month of January looks like.

January 2016

  1. Kip Omaha's Crowd Funding Campaign:

    Kip Omaha is an amateur science web site that offers web apps via your own secure portal or through one of our special programs or drives. A large part of what Kip does is for free (Not for profit*), our product sales and promotional drives are designed to help our bottom line. Even after that we still need help especially as we get started up. Go see our goFundMe.com page if you want to make a contribution to our cause or make a contribution via paypal through kipOmaha.org/donate.html.

    Event starts, January 8, 2016, and ends February 29, 2016.

    *   Currently going through the process of recieving 501 3c status.

  2. Kip Omaha's Drive for Drives:

    The Drive for Drives is a program designed to help students get a much needed thumb drive. Here at Kip we take unwanted thumb drives and turn them into something new and special. Each thumb drive is wiped clean and packaged with our IDE program for students. Kip offers the drives for free but we put out collection boxes so our supporters can easily donate to our cause.
    If your interested in seeing a list of vendors that participate in the program click here.

    Program begins January 11, 2016.

  3. Javascript Snippets:

    Javascript Snippets is our lite weight IDE made especially for javascript. It comes in two forms, the first is as a free download but has paid for extensions that you can purchase in app, and the second is a paid for online application that requires a user name and password.

    Application will be available Feb 15, 2016.

  4. Kip Forum Opens with Inline JSON

    Our forum's first ever topic is Inline JSON. Other forum topics will be posted as we finish them so stay posted and join in with the talk. Visit our forum at www.kipOmaha.org/forums.html

    Forum discussion begins January 11, 2016.
