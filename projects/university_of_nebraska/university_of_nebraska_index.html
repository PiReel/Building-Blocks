KipOmaha.org is an effort to bring an online tool that is useful for the creative thinkers and problem solvers.
Welcome to Kip's University of Nebraska Outreach Project Page.
Kip is proud to announce that we are now offering help to student and faculty clubs who are geared toward science and engineering. Through Kip Omaha, science and engineering clubs will be able to raise money, collaborate online, and use powerful web tools to build there club the way they envision in it.

Other goals of our outreach:
  1. Support alternative transportation education.
  2. Support eduction for web literacy.
